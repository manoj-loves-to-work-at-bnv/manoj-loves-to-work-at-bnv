      Indo-Japanese Synergies & Investment Thesis

      The collaboration between India's scale and frugal innovation with Japan's advanced technology and capital presents a powerful investment thesis. A Japanese VC in India is uniquely positioned to bridge these ecosystems.

      India's Strengths

      • Vast domestic market & talent
      • Low-cost manufacturing & R&D
      • "Frugal innovation" mindset
      • Rapid digitalization
      → ←

      Japan's Strengths

      • Deep capital pools & patient capital
      • Advanced manufacturing & robotics
      • Expertise in high-precision tech
      • Access to mature global markets

      The Opportunity

      Invest in Indian startups and provide them with Japanese technology, capital, and mentorship for global expansion. Conversely, help Japanese companies leverage Indian talent and market access for cost-effective scaling and new product development.
